# Hardware Component Specifications

### 1. Bio-inspired eSkin
- **Material:** Medical-grade silicone with embedded carbon nanotubes.
- **Sensing:** Piezoresistive pressure sensors (Sensitivity: 5g - 150kg).
- **Thermal:** Integrated heating mesh (Target: 37°C).

### 2. Actuators
- **Type:** Series Elastic Actuators (SEA).
- **Benefit:** Allows "Compliant Control"—the robot feels "soft" and reacts to patient force rather than being a rigid machine.

### 3. Visual Interface
- **Primary:** 12-inch Curved OLED for expressions and status.
- **Immersive:** Ultra-lightweight AR Glasses or Mini-Projector for floor interaction.
