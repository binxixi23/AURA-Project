# Ethical Use & Liability Disclaimer

### 1. Responsibility Framework
As a Multi-Agent system, AURA operates under a strict hierarchy of accountability:
- **Design Intent:** AURA is intended as a supplemental rehabilitation aid, not a replacement for professional medical supervision.
- **Fail-Safe Mechanism:** The system is designed with a "Hardware-Override-Software" principle. If the AI logic (Orchestrator) fails, the hard-coded Kinematic Safety Protocols take immediate control.

### 2. Liability Disclaimer (MIT License Context)
This project is provided **"as-is"**. While we strive for absolute safety through simulations and stress testing, the creators and contributors are not liable for:
- Improper assembly or hardware modification.
- Use of the software in non-certified environments.
- Injuries resulting from neglect of maintenance or hardware degradation.

### 3. Data Privacy & Patient Dignity
- **Affective Computing:** Emotional data detected by the robot must be processed locally (Edge AI) and never uploaded to public clouds to protect patient privacy.
- **Storytelling Integrity:** The AI must not generate content that is emotionally manipulative or medically misleading.

### 4. Who Is At Fault?
To ensure transparency, AURA includes a **"Black Box" Logging System**. In the event of a failure, the logs will identify if the root cause was:
- **Software Logic:** Errors in the Orchestrator's decision-making.
- **Hardware Failure:** Mechanical or sensor degradation.
- **Supervisory Error:** Misuse or neglect of safety warnings by caregivers.
